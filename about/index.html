About

I'm an aspiring software engineer and have a passion for building things that are useful to others. Technology has improved our lives in so many different ways and I want to help contribute to that.

I also enjoy experimenting with photography and capturing moments that feel natural and authentic. By taking pictures of moments that happen spontaneously, I feel that it will allow us to relive and reflect better on those moments, and understand one another as well.
